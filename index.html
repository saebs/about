About Me

Sabelo Ntabeni is a problem solver, learner, husband, and father of three Powerpuff girls. After obtaining my International Diploma In Computing in 2011, I joined a Payroll Software Company in early 2012. My time at the company has entailed working in multiple roles over eight years as a Software Support Analyst, Analyst Programmer. and Business Developer. My special interest area is building software artifacts and automation tools. As a professional, my approach is to aim for the trifecta of Computer Science Theory, Practice, and Software Engineering Economy in my work. I love to get my hands dirty in code in one form or the other. My bad coding habits are learning uncommon languages such as F# and Rust.
